Stages involved in the tendering process
•Preparation of  tender document (client responsibility)
•Invitation for bids (it is usually pasted in the news papers)
•Collection of  the tender document and preparation of  bids by bidders
•Bidsubmission(Thebidsaresubmittedinsealedenvelopes,thesebidsare
deliveredonthetimeanddatespecified.Anybidreceivedbytheclientafter
thedeadlinewillbereturnedunopenedtothebidder)
•Opening of  bids
(The client  opens the bids in the presence of  bidder’s representatives who 
choose to attend at the timeand placespecified)
•Evaluation and selection of  a successful bidder
(In order to qualify for the award of  a contract, a bidder must fulfill the 
criteria for selection specified) 
•Processing the “Notification of Intention to Award”
•Processing the “letter of acceptance” and “contract agreement”
•Award of  contract

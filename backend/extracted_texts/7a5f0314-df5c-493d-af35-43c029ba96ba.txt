CALL FOR APPLICATIONS | NRC UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS 2026 - 2027
Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources (LUANAR) is 
inviting applications from suitably qualified persons for admission into its 
Natural Resources College undergraduate programmes for the 2026-27 
academic year.
Candidates who were withdrawn from LUANAR or any other Public University on academic 
grounds and those already registered with LUANAR, or any comparable universities, are not 
eligible for admission.
All Undergraduate Diploma programmes are for 3 years (6 semesters).
• Undergraduate Diploma programmes tuition fees is MK650, 000 per academic year or 
MK325, 000 per semester. At least 50% of the tuition fee must be paid for a student to be 
registered.
• All admissions shall be on a self-sponsored basis hence candidates should be prepared to 
meet all the other costs associated with their studies.

Introduction

What is it all about?
•Food Chemistry is the study of chemical 
processes and interactions of all biological and 
non-biological components of foods.
•The study of analytical, biochemical, physical, 
nutritional and toxicological aspects of foods.
–It aim at understanding the molecular basis of the 
reactions in food.

•Many of the chemicals found in food occur 
naturally and include nutrients such as 
–proteins,
–carbohydrates,
–fats, 
–fibreand 
–host of other elements and compounds.
•Food chemistry covers the basic composition, 
structure and properties of foods and chemistry 
changes occurring during processing and 
utilization.

Soil colloids aretiny, chemically active soil particles, primarily 
composed of clay minerals and organic matter, that play a crucial role 
in soil fertility, nutrient retention, and overall soil properties

























Mineral Colloids
•Layer silicate clays
•1:1 clays (Kaolinite)
•2:1 clays (Micas, Illite, Vermiculite, Montmorillonite)
•2:1:1 clays (chlorite)

Organic Colloids
•Mostly soil “humus”, the chemically resistant 
organic matter in soils, that results from organic 
matter decomposition.
•Characteristics:  variably charged (usually -), high 
cation exchange capacity (CEC)

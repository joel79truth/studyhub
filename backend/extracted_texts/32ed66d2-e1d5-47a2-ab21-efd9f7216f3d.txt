Controlled Atmospheric 
storage
ï‚´Fruit life can be extended further by both refrigeration

ï‚´oxygen is kept at about 5 percent and 
ï‚´carbon dioxide at 1 to 3 percent, 
ï‚´while temperature is held at a level best suited to the

ï‚´Both dry commodities and fresh fruit and vegetables

Controlled Atmospheric 
storage
ï‚´Ripening is delayed by reducing the level of oxygen 
and increasing that of carbon dioxide in the cool 
storage room so that the respiration is reduced. 
ï‚´Under controlled atmosphere conditions the quality 
and the freshness of fruit and vegetables are retained
ï‚´Remember: The long-term storage of vegetables and 
fruit involves inhibiting the ripening and ageing 
processes, thus retaining flavor and quality. 

Importance of Controlled 
Atmospheric storage
a.A considerable decrease in respiration rate, with a 
reduction in climacteric maximum, accompanied by 
an expansion of both pre-climacteric and post-
climacteric periods
b.A reduction in the effect of ethylene on metabolism 
due to the interaction of O2 with ethylene, with a 
consequent delay of appearance of senescence 
symptoms
c.An extension in storage life, which can even be 
doubled, in as much as the over ripening is delayed

Importance of Controlled 
Atmospheric storage
d.The preservation of an excellent firmness of flesh, due 
to effect of CO2 concentration on the enzymes 
acting on cellular membranes
e.A high turgidity is achieved, such that fruits are more 
juicy and crisp
f.A smaller loss of acidity, sugars and vitamin C, so that 
the nutritional and sensory quality is higher
g.A limited degradation of chlorophyll, with a 
consequent higher stability of colour

Importance of Controlled 
Atmospheric storage
h.Some physiological alterations, such as chill  injuries, 
spot, decay, browning, water core and scald are 
prevented, or greatly limited
i.Moulds can be reduced, in particular under low O 2, 
high CO2 atmospheres
j.A longer shelf life in the post storage trading, which 
can even be trebled thanks to the protraction of the 
effects on respiration and on the other metabolic 
activities.

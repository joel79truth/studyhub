MODES OF POLLINATION
•Selfpollination
•Pollen from the anthers is transferred to the stigma of the 
same flower, resulting in fertilization. 
•Crosspollination
•Pollen from anthers is transferred to the stigma of another 
flower to cause fertilization. 
•The flowers can be on the same or different plants. 
•Usually by wind (anemophily) or insects (entomophily).

Population size and composition 
Populationsizehasthreefaces:
1.  Absolute Size: 
•This refers to the total number of individuals in a population. 
•It gives a straightforward count of the people living in a specific area or 
region at a given time.
2. Density: 
•This refers to the number of individuals per unit of area (e.g., per square 
kilometre). 
•Population density provides insights into how crowded or dispersed a 
population is, which can impact infrastructure, resources, and quality of 
life.
